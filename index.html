About Me

Code, craft &
creative vision

Hi, I'm Muhammed Can YALÇIN — a versatile software developer with a passion for bringing ideas to life. For over 6 years I've specialized in the Unity Game Engine, frequently leading projects as the sole developer end-to-end, from concept through deployment.

My portfolio spans VR/AR applications on Meta Quest, HTC Vive, PlayStation VR and Microsoft HoloLens; mobile & desktop games; and an ever-growing catalog of AI-generated music produced under the project name Shape of Void.

I believe the intersection of technology and art is where the most interesting things get built — and I'm always looking for the next frontier to explore.
